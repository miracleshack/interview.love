Tyler James Interview

The spotlight's on Tyler:

Have you heard of Jazztronica?

Ughhh, I've heard of Jazz but not Jazztronica.

I've offered you a free iPhone and you rejected. Why?

I'm just used to using Androids and I like to stick with what I'm familiar with. I honestly don't think Androids are that bad, and I think iPhones aren't that great.

I asked to interview you because you just asked me if I knew Marilyn Manson wrote the Bible, and told me that's a fact. What makes you ask and think this?

The fact that you told me I haven't read the entire Bible.

What's the happiest Marilyn Manson song you know of?

Oh... man, I'd have to think... He's got to have at least one happy song right? "I don't like drugs but the drugs like me."

Do you like Marilyn Manson?

Yea, I like some of his music, some of it's really good but the majority of it's trash.

Do you like Twitter user @MarilynTrashBin?

Yes, I think it sounds like a Twitter parody I'd do when I was a kid.

Does some of his music put you down, in a bad mood?

Um, I mean, when I was younger some of his music used to make me depressed but the music made me think about some of the things I didn't think about. Music is art, and art is supposed to make you think. If the music makes you think, the artist's doing their job.

What's your favorite Bible verse?

My favorite Bible verse is John 3:16 because there's a parody by wrestler Stone Cold.

What's your least favorite Bible verse?

I couldn't answer because I don't know of any you just listed.

Harry Potter or the Bible? Which is more scary?

Oh, I'd say the Bible. Christians freak me out. I mean, they tell you you go to Hell if you do bad stuff. You know what? I'm talking a lot of s*** but I haven't even read the Bible.

Do you feel everyone does bad stuff, and how no one can deserve Heaven is a subject meant to humble the prideful?

Yes, I think everyone has there own closets and demons and I think Heaven was made to keep bad people from doing bad things and to keep good people to keep doing good things... which isn't bad. Obviously it works, unlike Islam which twists ideology and flies planes into towers and s*** like that.

Okay, I'm being very biased with my questions, for my last, most biased question... Do you recall any idea that Heaven can't be earned by doing good things and everyone would be going to Hell without the free gift sacrifice on the cross?

I have heard that Heaven can't be earned but I've also heard the contrary.
