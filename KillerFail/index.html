KillerFail Interview

The spotlight's on Killer:

Who's your favorite champion to play? Which one has your favorite abilities? Lastly, which champ do you think is the least powerful, and then overpowered?

Um, I think my favorite champion to play probably right now and for a while has been Akali. She's a lot of fun and has my favorite abilities. She has a lot of movement, and I like having a champion that doesn't have to deal with mana.

The least powerful and overpowered? Least powerful... maybe Yuumi... who else, I can't think of another champion.

For most overpowered, I don't know, that changes all the time depending on the meta. Right now it seems like Xin Chao, Katarina... the two that come to mind. I play a lot of Samira, she's pretty OP and overpowered right now.

What's the most annoying champion in League?

I don't know...a lot of these, there's so many champions in League and it depends on who's playing them, too. I've been playing a little over a year now and I play in low elo. I have wild, different experiences all the time in low ego. Maybe Veigar because of the cc baby cage and he also has really strong abilities. Katarina, Swain, Blitzcrank, that can be absolute H***. But even Blitzcrank, if you can dodge, he can only hook so often.
